# Event Privacy Protocol

*Appendix · 18*

The standard privacy poster for any Logos event or Circle. Same format every time (Events, Rule 1). Posted at every entrance and stated at the open of each session. Only the location changes.

```markdown
# Privacy Protocol
## {{event · location · month}}

Privacy is the condition that makes free association possible. For us it is a requirement, not a feature. We state our posture before we begin so everyone here can take part without surveillance or exposure.

- **Recorded:** nothing by default. The Quiet zone has no cameras, recording, or broadcasting.
- **Consent:** ask before showing anyone's face. No names or doxing without it.
- **Blurred by default:** faces, where consent is not given.
- **Not shared:** no attendee lists, no names without consent.
```
