Civil society is in decline. Our institutions are failing. Our money is debased. People are atomised, indebted, and increasingly unable to provide for themselves or their families. Reform from within cannot reverse this because the system selects for adherence, and centralisation leads inevitably to corruption.

Debt

We were born into a debt we did not take on, for spending we did not approve, on terms we cannot refuse. Bills compound and interest accrues. The generation that took out this loan will not be the one to pay it.

Global government debt has climbed to a record $111 trillion in 2025, rising by $8.3 trillion in a single year.

The average millennial in the US has 30% less wealth than the average Boomer by age 35.

3.4 billion people now live in countries that spend more on interest payments than on either health or education.

Just 27% of Gen Z and 55% of millennials own a home, compared to 80% of Baby Boomers.

Surveillance

We are the most watched generation in human history. Every click, every step, every conversation is harvested, stored, and sold without our consent. Privacy is no longer the default. It's a privilege you have to fight for.

There are now ~1 billion surveillance cameras installed worldwide. More than 1 for every 8 people on Earth.

75% of police forces globally now have access to facial recognition technology.

Governments now legally purchase massive quantities of citizens' personal data from commercial brokers.

Corruption

Policy is no longer written for the public. It is written for whoever can afford the lobbyists, the donations, and the seat at the table. This is not a conspiracy. It is the operating model.

Billionaires are now estimated to be 4,000 times more likely to hold political office than ordinary citizens.

In the US alone, federal lobbying smashed records in 2025, with corporations and interest groups spending $5.08 billion in a single year to influence Washington.

Two-thirds of Americans (67%) now believe their federal government is "corrupt", and trust in government has crashed to just 17%.

Stagnation

Productivity is up. Education is up. Wages are flat. The wealth is being created but it is being captured upstream, by asset holders, financial intermediaries, and the institutions closest to the money printer. The distribution is not an accident. It is the design.

The 12 richest billionaires now own more wealth than the poorest half of humanity — 4 billion people.

Millennials work in an economy 70% more productive than when boomers were the same age — yet their median compensation has barely moved.

Basecamp The local launcher for the Logos stack

Install

Basecamp is a ready-to-run Logos distribution that you deploy on your own hardware, under your control. From the moment it’s installed, you’re standing on your own ground, not connecting to someone else’s infrastructure. The Logos runtime runs locally, core modules are loaded, and essential applications — wallet, messenger, file sharing, blockchain explorer — are ready to use from day one.
